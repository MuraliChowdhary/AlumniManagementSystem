Fundraising Initiatives

Fundraising Initiatives:

The alumni club engages in various fundraising efforts to support the alma mater's continued growth and success. These initiatives include scholarship funds, program endowments, facility enhancements, alumni legacy campaigns, annual giving campaigns, matching gift programs, and special events/fundraisers.

Key Features:


Scholarship Funds:

Support students in need by contributing to scholarship funds.

Program Endowments:

Provide long-term support for academic initiatives by endowing programs.

Facilities Enhancement:

Improve campus facilities for a better learning experience.

Alumni Association Grants:

Foster innovation with grants for student, faculty, or staff projects.

Alumni Legacy Campaigns:

Leave a lasting impact through planned gifts or bequests.

Annual Giving Campaigns:

Encourage regular contributions to support various priorities.

Matching Gift Programs:

Partner with employers to double or triple alumni donations.

Special Events/Fundraisers:

Host events like galas, auctions, or golf tournaments to raise funds
